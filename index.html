Hacker News

Hacker News is a popular news aggregation site that is often unfriendly towards views that don't fall in line with heteronormative patriarchy. Its moderation standards are entirely opaque and beyond reproach.

Alternatives

lobste.rs — Featuring an entirely open source, transparent moderation policy, and small but active community, lobste.rs is my go-to Hacker News replacement.

how to block

the-list is easily added to your own hosts file by curling the-lists's text file.

By running this command in your terminal, the contents of list.txt will be appended to your /etc/hosts file.

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/llkats/the-list/master/list.txt >> /etc/hosts

The flags are there to handle error cases more gracefully and safely handle redirects. See man curl for a more comprehensive explanation.
